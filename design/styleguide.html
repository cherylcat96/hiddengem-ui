Brand identity

HiddenGem's visual identity is clean, trustworthy, and community-oriented. A single dominant green signals nature, discovery, and authenticity. The display typeface (DM Serif Display) adds warmth and editorial character to headings, while DM Sans handles all UI copy.

Typography

Two typefaces: DM Serif Display for page titles and hero headings (editorial warmth), and DM Sans for all UI copy, labels, and body text. Both available free on Google Fonts.

Fallback stack: 'DM Sans', -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif
